About Tech Help with ❤️

Tech Help with ❤️ provides in-home tech support for seniors who want clear, patient, and reliable help with their devices.

I help with computers, printers, Wi-Fi, TVs, phones, email, and smart devices—whether something is broken, confusing, or just needs to be set up the right way.

My goal is simple: make technology feel less frustrating and more manageable, without jargon or pressure.

How I Help

I offer one-on-one support tailored to each client’s needs. That can mean troubleshooting a problem, setting up a new device, walking through steps slowly, or writing down clear notes so everything is easier to remember later.

Clients often call for help with printing, internet issues, email problems, smart TVs, streaming apps, new phones, and general computer support.

What Makes This Different

Tech support should not feel rushed, confusing, or overwhelming. I focus on patient, respectful help that builds confidence instead of making people feel behind.
